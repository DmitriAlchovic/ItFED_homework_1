It Is Wednesday My Dudes

"It Is Wednesday My Dudes" refers to an exploitable image of a Budgett's frog[1] paired with the text "It is Wednesday, my dudes." The image is frequently parodied due to the comedic look of the frog, which is also well-known for its unusual vocalizations.
